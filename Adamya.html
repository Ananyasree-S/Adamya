ADAMYA

Adamya is a mental health care site developed for people who deal with loneliness , stress , anxiety and such issues. It is a place where you can discover calming exercises , yoga , meditation and instrumental musics all in one place.
Since we all have been staying indoors for more than a year because of the pandemic, it becomes crucial for all of us to maintain proper mental health along with physical health. Keeping up with school and work during such a stressful time can be very exhausting. Thus we have created this page that can help you in finding calm in this chaos.
